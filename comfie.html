About Us

Welcome to Comfie, where comfort meets style in the art of interior design. As a proud brand under NiuHome, we specialize in crafting modern minimalist interiors that blend functionality with aesthetics. Our mission is to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary living experiences tailored to your unique lifestyle and preferences.

With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we have successfully completed over 10+ projects and curated a collection of 500+ products that embody elegance and practicality. Recognized as a leader in interior design, we take pride in being ranked 1st in Style Rankings.

At Comfie, we believe that your space is a reflection of your personality. That's why we collaborate closely with our clients to create designs that resonate with their vision and bring their dream spaces to life. Whether it's your home, office, or any other space, we are here to elevate your environment with designs that feel as good as they look. Let us turn your vision into reality and make every space truly yours. Welcome to the world of Comfie where every detail is designed for comfort and beauty.
